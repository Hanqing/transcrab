Federal Reserve Board Finance and Economics Discussion Series 2026-019

A User's Guide to Reducing the Federal Reserve's Balance Sheet

Authors: Alyssa G. Anderson, Alessandro Barbarino, Anthony M. Diercks, Stephen Miran

Abstract: This paper presents and analyzes a variety of options for reducing the Federal Reserve's balance sheet. Combined, these options open the door to balance sheet reduction of $1.2 to $2.1 trillion within the Fed's current ample reserves framework.
